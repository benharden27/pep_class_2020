    2.2 Seasonal Cycle

    We saw in our class on Temperture and Salinity, that the mixed-layer at mid-latitudes increases in depth during the winter due to strong, cold winds, before shoaling in the spring and into the summer when a strong thermocline (pycnocline) is developed.
